Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

+

You can track your order in your account dashboard under "Order History." Enter your order number or use the tracking link sent to your email.

What is your return policy?

+

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with tags attached.

How do I change or cancel my order?

+

You can change or cancel your order before it ships by contacting our support team via chat or email.

How do I use a discount code?

+

At checkout, you can enter your discount code in the "Promo Code" field. Once applied, the discount will be reflected in your total amount.
